Details

Olive was the youngest child of Francis Clement Villiers SANDERSON and Emma Stanton TORTOISE who lived in Port Augusta. She had 9 siblings. She married Warren Back (24 Mar 1889 - 18 Jul 1976) and they had two children, Douglas(1929-1983) and Mary (1927-1990). Her niece Jenny, daughter of her brother Verner, adds some more information. "Olive lived next-door to her sister, Ella Munro, who was the oldest in the family, in adjoining houses at Port Augusta West - the last two houses on the left. on the old road to Whyalla".
