Olive Aseneth SANDERSON


Birth date: July 11 1890

Death date: Sep 29 1946
Death place: Port Augusta West, South Australia, Australia
Burial:: Happy Valley Cemetery, South Australia.
Parents: Francis Clement Villiers SANDERSON and Emma Stanton TORTOISE
Married: Warren BACK (Sep 30 1925)
Children: Douglas, Mary

Details

Olive was the youngest child of Francis Clement Villiers SANDERSON and Emma Stanton TORTOISE who lived in Port Augusta. She had 9 siblings. She married Warren Back (24 Mar 1889 - 18 Jul 1976) and they had two children, Douglas(1929-1983) and Mary (1927-1990).

Her niece Jenny, daughter of her brother Verner, adds some more information. "Olive lived next-door to her sister, Ella Munro, who was the oldest in the family, in adjoining houses at Port Augusta West - the last two houses on the left. on the old road to Whyalla".
