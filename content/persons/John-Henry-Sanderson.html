John Henry SANDERSON


Birth date: 1825
Birth place: Newcastle upon Tyne, Northumberland, England
Christening: 19 Jan 1825, Newcastle upon Tyne, Northumberland, England, United Kingdom

Details

From the book by Douglas Sanderson

Henry worked in the family business in Newcastle before emigrating, and moved to Sydney soon after arrival in Adelaide. He married and had five children, three sons, Henry, John Frederick and two daughters, Florence and Beatrice. No research has been attempted to obtain information about his family.

His name is recorded as Henry in "England and Wales Census, 1841" and John Henry, in "England Births and Christenings, 1538-1975".
