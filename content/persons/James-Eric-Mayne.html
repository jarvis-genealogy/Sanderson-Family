James Eric MAYNE


Birth date: 22 May 1892
Birth place: North Adelaide, South Australia
Death date: 20 August 1942 (aged 50)
Death place: Goolwa, South Australia, Australia
Burial:: North Road Cemetery, Nailsworth, South Australia
Parents: Son of Edward Mayne and Edith Ferguson Sanderson

Details

James Eric Mayne was born on 22 May 1892, in North Adelaide, South Australia, Australia. His father, Edward Mayne, was 34 and his mother, Edith Ferguson Sanderson, was 33.

He immigrated to London, England, United Kingdom on 14 February 1929 on the "Berrima". South Australia, Immigrants Ship Papers, 1849-1940, show him as retired and 38 years old with a wife Mona aged 34 and daughter Ailsa aged 6 and Francis aged 3 years.

Further Shipping records, indicate his return on 7 Oct 1938 on the "Largs Bay", and show him as a pensioner aged 46 with a wife Mona aged 44 and daughter Ailsa Jean, student, aged 16 and son Francis James aged 14.

He died on 20 August 1942, in Goolwa, South Australia, Australia, at the age of 50, and was buried in North Road Cemetery, Nailsworth, South Australia, Australia.
