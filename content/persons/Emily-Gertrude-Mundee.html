Details

From The Chronicle (Adelaide, S.A.), Australia, Jan 13, 1938, Page 16.

Mrs. E. Sanderson
Mrs. Emily Sanderson, widow of Mr. Arthur Sanderson, who died in Adelaide on January 2, went to Port Lincoln about 30 years ago, and Mr. Sanderson engaged in the saddlery business with Mr. Allan Wood. Since the death of her husband, Mrs. Sanderson conducted a small grocery business in Liverpool street, Port Lincoln, until about a year ago, when she retired. Shortly before Christmas, with her daughter Gwen, who was transferred from the Port Lincoln to the Adelaide office of Messrs Reid Bros. Ltd., she moved to Adelaide. Five daughters -- Mesdames J. Owen (Cummins), F. Brown (Woodside), Doudle (Coulta), H. Hutchins (Port Lincoln) and Miss Gwen Sanderson survive.
