George Francis SANDERSON


Birth date: Circa Sep 19 1828
Birth place: Newcastle-on-Tyne, Northumberland, England
Death date: Circa May 16 1837 (at age ‎~8‏)
Death place: Leazes Tce, Newcastle-on-Tyne, England


Details

George is not mentioned in the book by Douglas Sanderson because he died when less than 8 years old in 1837 and did not travel to Australia with the family in 1852. However, Aunty Ella's writings refer to 7 children, including George. He is also listed in the family tree by Margaret Taylor.
